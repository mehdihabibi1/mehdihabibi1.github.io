" Biography.. "

Hi, I’m Mehdi Habibi, an Iranian violinist and kamancheh player from Gorgan. Since childhood, music has been my voice to connect with hearts around the world. Blending classical and traditional sounds, my performances often feature emotional melodies paired with stunning nature visuals creating peaceful, immersive experiences. I'm deeply grateful to share this journey with you, and I hope my music finds a place in your heart.
